What's inside

Everything about
your home. Nothing missing.

Most homeowners can't tell you what flooring is in their house, when it was installed, or what paint colour is on the walls. Your Passport changes that — permanently, for every trade Hooomz touches.

◦
Materials and finishes
Every product installed in your home — brand, model, colour code, finish, and installation date. Touch up paint in three years without guessing. Match flooring if you ever extend a room.
◷
Warranty and lifespan tracking
Every product warranty logged with expiry dates and claim instructions. Know what's covered, for how long, and exactly what to do if something goes wrong.
✓
Maintenance guidance
What needs attention, when to do it, and whether it's a DIY task or a call to a professional. Built around what's actually in your home — not a generic checklist.
⚙
Crew and install documentation
Who worked in your home, what they were qualified to do, and what was completed at each stage. A full record of the people and the work — not just the result.
When you sell

stays with
the house.

When you sell your home, your Passport transfers to the new owner automatically. They inherit a complete record of everything that was done — every finish, every warranty, every maintenance note. For a buyer, that's not a nice-to-have. It's proof.
