The Process

Three steps. Zero guessing.

01

Discover

The Site Visit

We arrive at your home with an existing floor plan loaded into our scope tool. Over 45 minutes, we walk every room with you — confirming dimensions, identifying issues, and agreeing on scope together. You're not handed a quote later. You watch it get built.

  • Floor plan confirmed on-site
  • Every room walked and documented
  • Scope agreed before we leave
  • No vague scope — line items only

02

Estimate

The Scope & Quote

Within 48 hours you receive a fixed-price quote broken down by room and trade. Paint, flooring, trim — each line item tied to a specific product, quantity, and labour rate. No allowances. No "we'll figure it out."

  • Fixed price — not an estimate
  • Broken down by room and trade
  • Materials specified by brand, SKU, colour
  • Revision rounds included
03

Install

Trades On-Site

We coordinate and supervise every trade. You get daily progress updates through the same tool you saw at the site visit — real task statuses, material delivery confirmations, and photos as work is completed.

  • Trades coordinated by Hooomz
  • Daily status updates
  • Same tool, live data
  • Sign-off required before final invoice
